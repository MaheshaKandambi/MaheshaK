About Us

A celebration of Sri Lankan's culture diversity and natural environment MK Fast Food restaurant showcases ingredient led, contemporary cuisine that demostrates the highly skilled techniques and relentless drive for prefection that has Chef Hiruni's trademark.

Five time winner of Best Upscale Restaurant in Sri Lanka Magazine's Restaurant Awards, MK Fast Food Restaurant is glamorous and elegant, yet relaxed and welcoming with warm and attensive service. MK Fast Food Restaurant is located in the heart of the city at Colombo and Galle Road.

Open for breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner, with in bar dining menus and its own private rooms, MK Fast Food can deiver an array of dining experiences from power breakfast to after-work drinks.
